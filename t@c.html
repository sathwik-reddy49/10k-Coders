Based on the provided search results, here are some basic terms and conditions that can be included in a form:

        1. Purpose and Scope

        Define the purpose of the form and the relationship between the parties involved (e.g., user and business).
        Specify the scope of the agreement, including the services or products offered.

        2. Acceptance and Consent

        Require users to explicitly accept the terms and conditions by checking a box or clicking a button.
        Clearly state that by submitting the form, users agree to be bound by the terms and conditions.

        3. Use of Information

        Specify how user-provided information will be collected, stored, and used (e.g., privacy policy).
        Outline any limitations on the use or disclosure of user data.

        4. Intellectual Property

        Reserve all intellectual property rights to the business or organization providing the service or product.
        Specify any permitted uses of intellectual property by users.

        5. Liability and Warranty

        Disclaim any liability for damages or losses arising from the use of the service or product.
        Specify any warranties or representations made by the business or organization.

        6. Termination

        Outline the circumstances under which the agreement can be terminated (e.g., breach of terms, expiration).
        Specify any notice periods or procedures for termination.

        7. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

        Specify the governing law applicable to the agreement.
        Outline any dispute resolution procedures, such as arbitration or mediation.

        8. Amendments

        Reserve the right to modify the terms and conditions at any time.
        Specify how changes will be communicated to users (e.g., posting updates on the website).

        9. Entire Agreement

        Specify that the terms and conditions constitute the entire agreement between the parties.
        Outline any prior agreements or understandings that are superseded by these terms.

        10. Severability

        Specify that if any provision of the terms and conditions is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining
        provisions will remain in effect.

        Here is a sample template:

        Terms and Conditions

        1. Purpose and Scope: This agreement governs the use of [Service/Product Name] by [User Name] and outlines the
        terms and conditions of our relationship.

        2. Acceptance and Consent: By submitting this form, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to be
        bound by these terms and conditions.

        3. Use of Information: [Business/Organization Name] will collect, store, and use user-provided information in
        accordance with our privacy policy.

        4. Intellectual Property: [Business/Organization Name] reserves all intellectual property rights to
        [Service/Product Name]. Users are permitted to use [Service/Product Name] for personal, non-commercial purposes
        only.

        5. Liability and Warranty: [Business/Organization Name] disclaims any liability for damages or losses arising
        from the use of [Service/Product Name]. [Service/Product Name] is provided "as-is" and "as-available."

        6. Termination: This agreement may be terminated by [Business/Organization Name] upon [number] days' written
        notice. Users may terminate the agreement at any time by ceasing use of [Service/Product Name].

        7. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution: This agreement will be governed by and construed in accordance with
        [State/Country] law. Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through [dispute resolution
        process].

        8. Amendments: [Business/Organization Name] reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time.
        Changes will be communicated through [method of notification].

        9. Entire Agreement: These terms and conditions constitute the entire agreement between [User Name] and
        [Business/Organization Name]. Prior agreements or understandings are superseded by these terms.

        10. Severability: If any provision of these terms and conditions is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the
        remaining provisions will remain in effect.

        By submitting this form, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these terms and
        conditions.

        Note:** This is a basic template and should be reviewed and customized according to your specific business needs
        and legal requirements. It's recommended to consult with a legal expert to ensure compliance with applicable
        laws and regulations.
